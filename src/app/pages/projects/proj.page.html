Timely App
    REACT
    JAVA
    MySQL

Timely was my Bethel Tech's final project. As a team of 5 spanning 3 timezones (one being India), the goal was to present an application that would use the past 4 weeks of input sales to render a weeks worth of projected sales.

As a user, you could input the percentage for labor cost. This input value, rendered to a percentage multiplied by the forcasted sales, would generate your labor cost. This would enable you to see how much money in labor you have to spend without going over labor cost projections.
